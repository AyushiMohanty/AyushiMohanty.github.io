June 2022 - August 2022

* Summer Intern *
MIT Lincoln Laboratory, School of Engineering

Selected as 1 of 25 students nationally to the prestigious BWSI Medlytics summer program dedicated to Data Science & AI in Healthcare.

Built AI & ML models using Python and other coding languages to create algorithms that accurately detect diseases such as Hypothyroidism from patient data, Parkinson's from Convolutional Pose Machines, Breast Cancer from mammograms, sleep stages from physiological signals. My final capstone project was the development of the web app Boneafide which aims to detect the presence and operability of Osteosarcoma (bone cancer) from histological images. Working to publish and use in clinical setting.

June 2021 - June 2022

* Student Researcher *
Rice University

Conducted research to study the immunological mechanisms used by a living organism with the goal of finding the root causes of cancer and neurodegenerative diseases. Experiments were performed on the nematode C. elegans as the model organism in order to identify the effect of Germline Mutations in glp-4 and N2. Published papers and presented posters for project regarding the Identification and Functional Characterization of the Regulators of the ESRE Pathway, a novel mitochondrial stress pathway.
