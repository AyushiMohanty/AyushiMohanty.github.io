This is
Ayushi

Lifelong Learner. Changemaker. Charismatic Persona. Leader. Up for Anything & Everything.

My name is Ayushi Mohanty and I am a current senior at Carnegie Vanguard High School in Houston, Texas. My interests lie in STEM, especially at the intersection of computer science and scientific research. I believe science is utilitarian and am deeply passionate about educational and public health equity. I continue to immerse myself into the interdisciplinary fields of biochemistry, computational biology, and public health. Some of my highlighted works include Project Unbiased — a campaign to promote minority representation in data science, my research on improving the efficacy of cancer treatment using combined drug therapy, as well as Boneafide — the web app I developed in order to accurately diagnosis osteosarcoma from histological images.

Dancer

Indian Classical Dance - Odissi

The challenges of immersing myself into a different world here in the U.S. while balancing my Indian identity has been an experience that many immigrant children can relate to. However, for over 6 years Odissi has been a portal and allowed me to express my individuality in a unique manner.
